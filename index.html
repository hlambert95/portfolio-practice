Student Software Engineer

I’m a second-year student studying a Bachelor of Software Engineering with a major in Data Science at the University of Canberra. I have found a passion for creating efficient and aesthetic user interfaces to enhance the user experience.

In my career, I have had the privilege of working in the Operations teams for Apple and Tesla, and it was during my time at these tech giants that I discovered my love of leveraging technology to increase productivity. Previous to this, I worked and studied as a musician — writing, producing, and performing. I believe my experience gives me the ability to solve problems from a creative and professional perspective.

I love learning, I love to create, and I love solving problems to make people’s live easier. Thanks for visiting, and please get in touch if you’d like to chat!
